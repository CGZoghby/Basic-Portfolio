About Me

So today I found an adorable, tiny, little bunny (abbreviated TLB for ease), barely moving, huddled against a concrete sidewalk guard. This TLB had an issue that I was not disciplined enough to diagnose, but my heart fell for it and I had to take care of it.

There is a wildlife hospital near me, but it's crewed by volunteers and funded through donations; their voicemail said they were maxed out but passed me the number for animal control.

Enter Jim. Jim lives in my apartment complex and takes care of the multi-generational colony of feral cats living there. xJim found the bunny, and immediately pledged to take care of it and nurse it back to health. Jim is my hero and a champion for humanity.
